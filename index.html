The Problem

Trees weakened by disease and pests develop weak roots, leading to soil erosion and devastating landslides in areas like Teluk Bahang and Batu Ferringhi.

Our Solution

SmartLeaf combines AI-powered vision, environmental sensors, and instant alerts to detect tree diseases before they cause disasters.

AI Vision (HuskyLens)

Advanced camera with built-in AI detects visual symptoms of tree diseases, recognizing patterns invisible to the naked eye.

IoT Environmental Sensors

DHT22: Measures temperature and humidity around the tree.
Gas Sensor: Detects smoke or harmful gases from illegal burning.

Real-Time Alerts (Micro:bit + WiFi)

Micro:bit processes data and sends instant Telegram alerts to forestry teams when disease is detected, enabling rapid response.
