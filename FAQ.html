SmartLeaf is an AI-powered system that uses the HuskyLens camera to analyze leaf images and detect signs of disease, stress, or nutrient deficiency.

The HuskyLens captures a leaf image and sends it to an AI model that checks for colour changes, patterns, and spots.

SmartLeaf uses HuskyLens as the vision sensor and a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W to send results to a Telegram bot.

It processes detection results and sends real-time alerts via WiFi.

AI can detect small changes that humans miss, helping early diagnosis.

It helps farmers, environmental officers, PERHILITAN, and students monitor plant health efficiently.
